The Tale of We-Desi Chicken Biryani

Once upon a time, King BhanuPratap of Tastebudistan was bored with his usual feasts. He challenged his chefs to create a dish that would amaze him. After many kitchen disasters (including way too much chili), they finally made the perfect Chicken Biryani. It was so delicious that neighboring kingdoms wanted the recipe. Today, at We-Desi, we've perfected this legendary dish. So next time you savor our Chicken Biryani, remember: it's a story, but it shows how much you love Biryani. Enjoy!
